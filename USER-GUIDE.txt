HOW TO USE (FOR EMPLOYEES)
-----------------------------------

1. LOGIN
   - Go to the website
   - Enter your username and password
   - Click "Sign In"

2. COMPLETE TRAINING
   - Click "Training" in the top menu
   - Choose a module
   - Read through the content
   - Click "Next" to continue through pages

3. TAKE TESTS
   - Click "Testing" in the top menu
   - Select a test (only available after completing the module)
   - Answer all questions
   - Click "Submit Test"
   - View your results and download certificate if you passed

4. CHANGE LANGUAGE
   - Click the globe icon (top-right)
   - Choose: English, Tamil, Hindi, or Telugu

-----------------------------------
HOW TO USE (FOR ADMINS)
-----------------------------------

1. CREATE TRAINING MODULE
   - Go to "Admin" → "Modules" tab
   - Click "Create New Module"
   - Fill in title, description, and content
   - Click "Save"

2. CREATE TEST
   - Go to "Admin" → "Tests" tab
   - Click "Create New Test"
   - Add questions and answers
   - Mark correct answers
   - Click "Save"

3. REVIEW PROGRESS
   - Go to "Review" section
   - View all employee training progress
   - Check test scores and completion rates

-----------------------------------
IMPORTANT NOTES
-----------------------------------

• Your progress is saved in your browser automatically
• Use the same device/browser to see your progress
• If you clear browser data, you'll lose your progress
• Take your time - safety training is important!

-----------------------------------
TROUBLESHOOTING
-----------------------------------

Problem: Can't see the website / 404 error
Solution: Press Ctrl+Shift+R (or Cmd+Shift+R on Mac) to hard refresh

Problem: Lost my progress
Solution: Make sure you're using the same browser and device

Problem: Page won't load
Solution: Clear browser cache or try incognito mode
